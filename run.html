World Size Selection Slider:

Drag the slider to change the world scale.

Scale Multiplier:

  • 10: World is ten times larger
  •   4: World is four times larger
  • -1, 0 or 1: World is normal scale
  • -  4: World is four times smaller
  • -10: World ten times smaller
